About

Build Club builds confident makers.

Build Club is a student-led, volunteer-run educational initiative. It is not currently a registered charitable organization. All activities are non-commercial.

Mission

We help students discover how technology works by building real circuits, writing beginner-friendly code, and working on projects they can show at school and at home.

What we teach

Sessions cover low-voltage electronics, microcontrollers, and the fundamentals of programming with a focus on curiosity, safe experimentation, and teamwork.

How sessions run

Each session combines a short lesson with hands-on build time. Volunteers guide students through step-by-step activities, and participants work in small groups to test ideas and document results.
