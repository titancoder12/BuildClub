Privacy & Media Consent

Respecting student privacy.

We collect only the information needed to run sessions and communicate with families.

Data collection

We collect minimal information such as student name, grade, and a parent or guardian contact so we can coordinate sessions and safety updates.

We do not sell or share personal information.

Media consent

We do not publish student names or photos publicly without parent or guardian consent.

Families can opt out of photos or videos at any time by contacting us.

Student work ownership

Students own the projects they create and keep credit for their ideas, designs, and write-ups.
