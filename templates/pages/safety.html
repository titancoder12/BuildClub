Safety & Supervision

Safe, low-risk, and student-friendly.

Build Club activities are designed for beginners and focus on safe, low-voltage experimentation.

  • Low-voltage only (5V) projects for all student activities.
  • No soldering initially; students start with breadboards and modular components.
  • Adult supervision is present during in-person sessions.
  • Clear tool rules: keep parts organized, handle components gently, and ask before troubleshooting.

Safety questions

Families can request additional safety details or supervision plans by emailing buildclub.org@gmail.com.
