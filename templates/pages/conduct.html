Code of Conduct

A welcoming space for every student.

Build Club is committed to respect, inclusivity, and collaboration in every session.

  • Respect each other and listen actively during lessons and builds.
  • Be inclusive and supportive of different skill levels and backgrounds.
  • Collaborate, share tools, and give credit to teammates.
  • No harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind.
  • Report concerns to a volunteer lead or email us directly.

Reporting

Please report any issues or questions to buildclub.org@gmail.com.
